Our First Valentine's Day

Dear Journal,

Today was our first Valentine's Day together, and I wanted everything to be perfect. I spent weeks planning a surprise for Vazeeda.

The look on her face when she saw the candlelit dinner I had prepared was worth all the effort. We danced in the living room to our favorite songs, and I gave her the necklace I had been saving for this special day.

But the best moment was when we were just sitting together, talking about our dreams for the future. It's in those quiet moments that I feel most connected to her.

I'm falling deeper in love with her every day.

The Little Things

Dear Journal,

It's the little things about Vazeeda that make me fall in love with her more each day. The way she always remembers to ask about my day. How she brings me coffee in the morning just the way I like it. The cute text messages she sends when we're apart.

Today, she surprised me with tickets to see my favorite band. She's been saving for months, even though this kind of music isn't really her thing. That's just who she is - always thinking of others before herself.

I'm collecting all these small moments in my heart, creating a treasure trove of memories that I'll cherish forever.
